## Why this site exists

I learn best when I have to explain an idea clearly. Kevin's Lessons Learned is
where I turn questions, experiments, mistakes, and useful discoveries into
notes that I can revisit and others can use.

The process is deliberately public. A topic begins as a GitHub Issue, develops
through research and practice, and becomes a post only after I can explain what
changed in my understanding.

## What to expect

The subjects will follow what I am actively learning. Posts may be tutorials,
technical deep dives, project retrospectives, or short notes about a lesson
worth remembering. I will favour honest reasoning, reproducible examples, and
links to primary sources.

This is a living notebook, not a claim to have the final word.
